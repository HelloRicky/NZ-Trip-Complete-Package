## 💡 How to Open | 如何打开

**From Obsidian:**
1. Click any link above
2. Will open in your default browser
3. 点击上面的任何链接
4. 将在默认浏览器中打开

**Alternative:**
1. Right-click the link
2. Choose "Open in default app"
3. 右键点击链接
4. 选择"在默认应用中打开"
