## Money Saving Tips 💡

### Before You Go
- [ ] Book accommodation early (better rates)
- [ ] Look for combo deals (gondola + luge packages)
- [ ] Book activities online in advance (sometimes cheaper)
- [ ] Consider travel insurance excess to reduce premiums
- [ ] Download offline maps (save data costs)

### During the Trip
- [ ] Shop at supermarkets (Pak'nSave cheapest)
- [ ] Self-cater breakfasts (save $15-20/person/day)
- [ ] Pack lunches for day trips
- [ ] Fill up fuel in smaller towns (often cheaper)
- [ ] Free activities: beaches, walks, playgrounds, museums
- [ ] Tap water is safe to drink (bring reusable bottles)
- [ ] Look for kids eat free deals
- [ ] Share family meals at restaurants

### Splurge vs Save
**Worth the splurge:**
- Whale watching (once in lifetime!)
- Skyline Gondola + Luge (kids will remember forever)
- One special dinner in Queenstown

**Easy saves:**
- Skip paid hot pools (natural scenery is free!)
- Pack own snacks instead of buying at attractions
- Drive instead of fly Queenstown-Christchurch (save ~$700)

- 1 AUD = _____ NZD
- 1 NZD = _____ AUD

**Where to Exchange:**
- Use credit card for most purchases (usually best rate)
- Withdraw NZD cash from ATMs (check your bank's international fees)
- Notify your bank you're traveling to avoid card blocks
- Consider a travel card (Wise, Revolut) for better rates

**Credit Card Recommendations:**
- Use credit cards with no foreign transaction fees
- Always choose to pay in NZD (not AUD) when asked - better rate
