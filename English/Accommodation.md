### Camping Options in NZ

#### Option 1: Holiday Parks (Recommended with kids)
- **Facilities:** Showers, toilets, playgrounds, powered sites
- **Cost:** $30-60/night per site
- **Pros:** Kid-friendly, safe, facilities
- **Locations:** Every town has holiday parks

#### Option 2: DOC Campsites
- **Facilities:** Basic (toilets, sometimes water)
- **Cost:** $6-15/night per person
- **Pros:** Scenic locations, cheap
- **Cons:** Basic facilities, remote

#### Option 3: Freedom Camping
- **Facilities:** Must be self-contained
- **Cost:** Free (with certified self-contained vehicles)
- **Rules:** Only at designated freedom camping areas
- **Check:** Local council rules before parking

#### Option 4: Mix with Hotels
- **Strategy:** Camp some nights, hotels other nights
- **When to camp:** Shorter travel days, scenic locations
- **When hotel:** Long travel days, cities (Christchurch, Queenstown)
