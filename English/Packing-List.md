## Weather & Climate - April (Autumn)

**Temperature Range:** 10-18°C (50-64°F)
**Season:** Autumn (fall) - cooling down from summer
**Conditions:** Mild days, cool evenings, possible rain
**Layering:** Essential! Weather can change quickly

## 🚫 What NOT to Bring

### Restricted in New Zealand
- ❌ Fresh food (fruit, meat, dairy) - strict biosecurity
- ❌ Hiking boots with dirt (clean thoroughly before travel!)
- ❌ Honey products
- ❌ Seeds or plant material
- ❌ Camping equipment with soil

### Unnecessary Items
- ❌ Too many clothes (laundry facilities available)
- ❌ Hair dryer (provided by hotels)
- ❌ Beach towels (hot pools provide, or use hotel towels)
- ❌ Too many books (heavy!)

## 📦 Packing Tips

### General Tips
1. **Use packing cubes** - organize by person or category
2. **Roll clothes** instead of folding (saves space)
3. **Wear bulkiest items** on flight (jackets, boots)
4. **Pack a change of clothes** in carry-on (in case luggage delayed)
5. **Check airline baggage allowance** before packing
6. **Leave space for souvenirs** on return trip!

### Layering Strategy
- Base layer (thermal or t-shirt)
- Mid layer (fleece or sweater)
- Outer layer (waterproof jacket)
- This works for NZ autumn weather!

### For Families
- **Pack each child's clothes by outfit** (easier to grab in mornings)
- **Label everything** (especially kids' items)
- **Pack snacks from home** (familiar foods for picky eaters)
- **Bring small surprises** (new toys revealed during trip for boredom)
