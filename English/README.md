## 📱 Important Notes

### Weather (April)
- 🌡️ **Temperature:** 10-18°C (50-64°F)
- 🧥 **Pack:** Layers, rain jackets, warm clothes
- ☀️ **Sunscreen:** SPF50+ (NZ sun is strong!)

### Driving
- 🚗 **Drive on LEFT side** of road
- 👶 **Child seat required** for Jasmine (age 5)
- 🗺️ **Download offline maps**

### With Kids
- 💊 **Motion sickness tablets** - essential for whale watching!
- 🍭 **Pack snacks** - long drives
- 🎮 **Download entertainment** - tablets, movies, games
- ⏰ **Early starts** - Day 2 & 3 are long driving days

### Time Zone
- ⏰ **NZ is 2 hours ahead** of Sydney in April
- All times in itinerary are NZ local time
